# Financial_Dashboard_1
Technology Used - PowerBI

Elaborated Overall Explanation of the Financial Dashboard

This Financial Dashboard is designed to give a complete 360-degree view of the company’s sales and financial performance. It transforms raw sales data into meaningful insights that help business leaders make informed decisions.
The dashboard highlights the most important KPIs—including total units sold, gross sales, and total profit—so anyone can instantly understand how well the company is performing at a high level.
Beyond the top KPIs, the dashboard breaks down the performance across different customer segments, product lines, and geographical regions. This makes it possible to identify:
Which customer group contributes the most revenue (e.g., Government and Small Business)
Which products are the top performers (e.g., Paseo, VTT, Velo)
Where sales are happening around the world, giving a clear sense of market reach
How sales fluctuate over time, helping identify growth opportunities and seasonal trends.

The inclusion of interactive filters (Year and Country) gives users the ability to explore the data from different angles. This makes the dashboard flexible and useful for multiple stakeholders—such as sales managers, financial analysts, and business decision-makers.
Ultimately, this dashboard helps answer major business questions like:

Which segments should we focus on more?
Which products require marketing or improvement?
Which regions should we target for expansion?
Are our profits increasing or decreasing over time?

By converting data into easy-to-read visualizations, this project demonstrates the ability to use Power BI for real business analytics, making it valuable in roles like Data Analyst, Business Analyst, or Financial Analyst.
